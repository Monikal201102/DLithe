Eyewitnesses reported hearing a loud explosion near the central power grid at approximately 6 AM. Officials are currently investigating the cause, though preliminary reports suggest a possible technical failure. Meanwhile, businesses, hospitals, and transport systems were heavily impacted, with authorities urging residents to remain patient as repair work continues.

“It was like the entire city suddenly went dark,” said Sarah Thompson, a local shop owner. “We had to close our store for half the day, and even traffic lights weren’t working.”

City officials have assured the public that power will be fully restored by evening, and an official report is expected to be released soon.
